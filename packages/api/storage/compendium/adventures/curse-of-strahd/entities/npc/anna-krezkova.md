Anna Krezkova is the burgomaster's wife, who plays a critical role in community rituals, such as overseeing the birth of new children. Her dedication to her family and village creates both conflict and motivation throughout the story.

## DM Notes
Her role as a leader may lead to conflicts with her husband's actions.
