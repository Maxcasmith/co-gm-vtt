Once a great warrior and ruler, the Vampyr, now a tortured undead, is consumed by his lost youth and his obsession with Tatyana, the woman he loves who spurned him. His rage and despair led him to commit fratricide on his brother Sergei, sealing a dark pact with death, and now he dwells in solitude within the depths of Ravenloft, forever chasing the remnants of a life he can no longer attain.
