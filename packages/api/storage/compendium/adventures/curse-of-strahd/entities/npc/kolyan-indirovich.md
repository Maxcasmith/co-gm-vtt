Kolyan Indirovich serves as the Burgomaster of Barovia, and though he seeks to protect his village, he is inadvertently ensnared in Strahd’s machinations. His correspondence with adventurers serves as bait, showcasing the intricate web of deception surrounding Strahd's influence.

Kolyan Indirovich serves as the burgomaster of the village of Barovia. His leadership is challenged by Strahd’s oppression, and he seeks to unite the Barovians against the vampire's tyranny, even while feeling the weight of impending doom.

## DM Notes
Kolyan is a valuable ally for players but might require persuasion to act, as he is caught between fear and hope.

Kolyan Indirovich is the Burgomaster of Barovia, dedicated to the safety of his village and the well-being of Ireena Kolyana, whom he loves dearly. With a heavy heart, he reaches out for help, offering all he has in exchange for her rescue. His desperation showcases both his love and the dire situation they face.
