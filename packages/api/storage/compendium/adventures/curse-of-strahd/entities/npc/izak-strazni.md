Izek is the baron's ruthless henchman, known for his violent tendencies and brutal ways. He wields an iron ring of keys, keeping a tight grip on the town's criminals.

Izek Strazni is a town guard who takes it upon himself to hunt down threats to Vallaki. His involvement in the hunt for the saber-toothed tiger shows both his determination and the brutality he is willing to employ.

Izek Strazni is a loyal enforcer of Strahd, known for his ruthless methods and unsettling calm. A master of intimidation, he embodies the terror that Strahd instills in his minions, enforcing the vampire lord's will across the land. Beneath his obedient facade, however, lurks an unstable mind driven by power and fear.
