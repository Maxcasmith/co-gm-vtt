Izek Strazni is the baron's henchman, known for his violent tendencies and fearsome appearance due to his monstrous arm. He maintains order in the town through intimidation and is a key enforcer for the burgomaster.

Izek Strazni is a large boy with a dark past, orphaned and without a soul. He has become a sociopath, driven by his twisted motivations and obsession with his lost sister, Ireena Kolyana. Though loyal to the burgomaster of Vallaki, he is known for his violent tendencies and collection of dolls resembling Ireena.
## DM Notes
Izek's connection to Ireena could lead to revealing their true relationship in the story.
