Baron Vargas Vallakovich is the burgomaster of Vallaki, who is determined to maintain control and create a false sense of security through festivals and festivities. His leadership style is contentious, and he often prioritizes personal image over the well-being of the villagers.

## DM Notes
Baron Vargas can be an ally or an obstacle, depending on player actions. His hidden agenda and potential for manipulation might lead to unexpected consequences.

Baron Vargas Vallakovich is deluded into thinking that if everyone in Vallaki is happy, they will be safe from Strahd. He stages festivals and arrests anyone who shows dissent.

The burgomaster of Vallaki, Baron Vargas Vallakovich is determined to maintain control over the town through festivals, which he believes protect the villagers from Strahd. He is deeply afraid of Lady Fiona Wachter and often reacts harshly to dissenters.
