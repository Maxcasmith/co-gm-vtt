Dmitri Krezkov is the burgomaster of Krezk. He is a stern lord who assumes the arrival of adventurers means trouble for his village. Although he is protective of his people, his sorrow over the loss of his son can sometimes lead to moments of vulnerability that can be exploited by the clever.
