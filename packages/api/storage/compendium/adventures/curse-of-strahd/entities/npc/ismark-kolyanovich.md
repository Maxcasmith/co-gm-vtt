Ismark (LG male human **veteran**) is a young man who sits by himself at a corner table, sipping his wine. Other villagers call him "Ismark the Lesser" because he has lived in the shadow of his father for most of his life.

Ismark invites the characters to join him, offers to pay for their wine, and asks for their aid in protecting his adopted sister, **Ireena Kolyana**. He wants the characters to help him escort Ireena to Vallaki, a settlement in the heart of the valley, beyond the view of Castle Ravenloft and (he hopes) beyond the reach of Strahd.

Ismark Kolyanovich rises as the new burgomaster of Barovia, embodying hope and determination for his community. Grateful to the characters, he seeks to forge a safer future in the wake of Strahd's demise.
