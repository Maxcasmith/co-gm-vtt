Gertruda is the sheltered daughter of Mad Mary, who has lived a life removed from the dangers of the outside world. Fascinated by the beauty of Castle Ravenloft, she can’t see the lurking peril of Strahd. Her innocence makes her a tragic character, as she remains unaware of the consequences of her choices.
