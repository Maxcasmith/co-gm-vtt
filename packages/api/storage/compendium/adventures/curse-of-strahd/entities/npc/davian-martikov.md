Davian Martikov is the patriarch of the Martikov family and a wereraven who passionately oversees the winemaking operations. He is deeply troubled by the recent troubles at the winery and harbors resentment towards his middle son, Urwin, whom he believes has betrayed him. Davian’s strong belief in the necessity of wine for the spirits of the Barovians drives his relentless fight to protect his family and business.

Davian Martikov is an old and suspicious man who is the current owner of the winery and vineyard. He initially doesn't share much about the stolen gems due to his mistrust but informs the characters about the threats faced by his family from evil druids and blights.

Davian Martikov is the owner of the Wizard of Wines Winery. He is fiercely protective of his family's legacy and the secrets of the winery, harboring knowledge about illegal wine shipments made to the vampire Strahd. Davian is currently focused on supporting his daughter Stefania and son-in-law Dag as they care for their new baby.

Davian is the leader of the Martikov family, deeply committed to protecting his winery and his family's legacy. He is grateful for the party's help in restoring the winery but is devastated by the destruction caused by Wintersplinter.
