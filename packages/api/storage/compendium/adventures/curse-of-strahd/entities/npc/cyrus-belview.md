desc: Cyrus Belview is a loyal servant of Strahd, tasked with guarding the Center Court Gate. He possesses knowledge of the command word to lift the portcullis, giving him a significant role in controlling access to the castle.

Cyrus Belview is a mongrelfolk and Strahd's faithful servant. He stands 4 feet, 9 inches tall and appears shorter due to his hunched posture. Despite his loyalty to Strahd, Cyrus displays signs of madness and often behaves in an erratic, comedic manner.

Cyrus Belview is a disfigured manservant of Strahd, carrying out his master's orders with a sense of loyalty born of fear. Despite his condition, he possesses invaluable information due to his proximity to the dark powers of Castle Ravenloft. His connection to Morgantha through the hag eye adds a layer of intrigue to his character.
## DM Notes
Cyrus might have knowledge of hidden aspects of Strahd's plans and could provide players with critical information if approached correctly.
