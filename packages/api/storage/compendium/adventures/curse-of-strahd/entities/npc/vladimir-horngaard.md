Vladimir Horngaard is a fallen hero who seeks redemption and the revival of his once-great order. Residing in Argynvostholt, he possesses knowledge of the treasures that can be used against Strahd.

Vladimir Horngaard, once the greatest knight of the Order of the Silver Dragon, returned as a revenant to avenge his fallen comrades. His obsession with revenge has led him to command a legion of revenant knights, fighting against Strahd's soldiers. However, his inability to let go of his hatred is a barrier to his and his knights' redemption.

Vladimir Horngaard is the commander of the fallen Order of the Silver Dragon, now a revenant bound by hatred and a desire for revenge against Strahd. His spirit embodies desperation and anger, and he warns intruders of the consequences of approaching him without respect or intention to help.

**Vladimir Horngaard** is a knight of the long-destroyed Order of the Silver Dragon and a revenant filled with vengeance against Strahd von Zarovich. Though he fought valiantly against the vampire's forces, he fell, along with his beloved fellow knight Sir Godfrey Gwilym. With his spirit now trapped in Barovia, he seeks to ensure Strahd suffers for his crimes, even at the cost of his own peace.
