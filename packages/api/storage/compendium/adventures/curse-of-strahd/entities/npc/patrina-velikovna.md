Patrina Velikovna is a dusk elf archmage and Kasimir's sister, recently resurrected through dark magic. Initially appearing repentant, she harbors ambitions to rejoin Strahd and plots revenge against Rahadin for the mutilation of her brother. Her struggle for power and autonomy drives her actions, making her a complex figure.

## DM Notes
Patrina’s transformation into a vampire spawn should be carefully managed, as it alters her relationship with both Strahd and the characters.
