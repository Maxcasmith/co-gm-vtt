Pidlwick II is an animated clockwork effigy designed to resemble a small man with a painted face. While he cannot speak, he understands Common and communicates through gestures. If treated well, he serves as a guide within Castle Ravenloft, though he harbors resentment against those who mistreat him, leading to mischievous consequences.
