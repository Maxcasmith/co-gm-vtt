Zelraun Roaringhorn is a Harper and a mage who arrives in Daggerford determined to aid the locals against the werewolf threat. He is resourceful and brave, offering silver for weapons and support for those willing to confront the lycanthropes. His network allows him to provide valuable resources in dire situations.
