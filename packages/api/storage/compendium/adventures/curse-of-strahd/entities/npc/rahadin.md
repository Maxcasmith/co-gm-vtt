If **Rahadin** was killed or captured in a previous encounter, this encounter doesn't occur. Otherwise, Strahd's mysterious chamberlain approaches quietly. A character whose passive Wisdom (Perception) score meets or exceeds **Rahadin**'s Dexterity (Stealth) check hear him.

>>"The master wishes to see you," intones a grim voice in the darkness.

**Rahadin** directs the characters to a random location in the castle, determined by rolling a d6:

|  d6 | Location                   |
|:---:|----------------------------|
|  1  | Chapel (area K15)          |
|  2  | Audience hall (area K25)   |
|  3  | Study (area K37)           |
|  4  | Tower roof (area K57)      |
|  5  | Wine cellar (area K63)     |
|  6  | Torture chamber (area K76) |

Strahd isn't actually at that location unless the card reading (see *chapter 1*) indicates that he is.

If the characters ask **Rahadin** to lead the way, he declines. If the characters ask for directions, he tells them whether they need to ascend, descend, or remain on the level they're at. If they attack him, he fights to the death. Otherwise, he doesn't leave until after they do.

Rahadin is an elf who serves as the chamberlain of Castle Ravenloft. He is stealthy and discreet, ensuring that any guests are welcomed properly while maintaining a vigilant watch over them. If he perceives any threat, he is prepared to fight to defend his master’s domain.

Rahadin is a significant character in the castle, serving Strahd with ruthless loyalty. He waits in the Chamberlain's Office for the characters to arrive, ready to eliminate them if they are not cautious.

Rahadin is Strahd's loyal chamberlain who comes to the Amber Temple to petition the dark god for the release of his master from torment. He rides a phantom steed and performs a dark sacrificial ritual during his visits, demonstrating his deep allegiance to Strahd. He is resolute in avoiding capture and will defend himself if confronted.

## DM Notes
Rahadin knows that Strahd will deal with the characters at his own discretion, making him a cautious adversary.

Rahadin, the dusk elf chamberlain, embodies unwavering loyalty to Strahd. Following his master's death, Rahadin is driven by grief to avenge him, demonstrating a powerful mix of rage and sorrow.

Rahadin is the enigmatic advisor to Strahd, possessing knowledge of the realm's darkest secrets. With a demeanor that radiates an unsettling calm, he orchestrates the plans and machinations of the vampire lord. While he appears to be entirely loyal, whispers suggest that Rahadin may have aspirations that could change the balance of power in Barovia.

**Rahadin**, the dusk elf chamberlain of Castle Ravenloft, serves Strahd's family faithfully for nearly five hundred years. A ruthless warrior and Strahd's eternal servant, he instills terror in the castle staff and has a dark past involving the downfall of the dusk elves. Rahadin's loyalty remains unwavering even after the tragedy of Tatyana, as he continues to execute Strahd's bidding with ruthless efficiency.
