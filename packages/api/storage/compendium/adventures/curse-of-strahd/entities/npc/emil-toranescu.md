A strong young man clutches the bars of his cell while struggling to keep his teeth from chattering. His clothes are shredded, and he is soaked from head to toe.
In truth, Strahd locked Emil here as punishment for causing a schism in his werewolf pack. Anxious to prove his worth to Strahd, Emil rewards the characters for freeing him by attacking them when a good opportunity arises. Emil doesn't turn against the characters if they claim to be allies of his wife, Zuleika.

Emil Toranescu is a werewolf who challenges the brutal leadership of Kiril Stoyanovich. He advocates for the lives of children taken by the pack, proposing a strategy to grow their numbers rather than eliminate them. His disagreements with Kiril have led to a significant rift in the pack, making him a key player in the emerging conflict.

## DM Notes
Emil's fate hangs in the balance, and his opposition to Kiril could be a pivotal storyline for the characters.

Emil Toranescu is a pivotal character in the werewolf den, possessing the ability to command the other werewolves not to attack characters in his presence. His fate is tied to the players’ actions.

Emil Toranescu is a cunning werewolf who seeks to undermine Kiril Stoyanovich and take control of the pack. He is determined and ambitious, willing to kill Kiril to achieve his goals. His intentions can create significant obstacles for the adventurers if they find themselves entangled in werewolf politics.
