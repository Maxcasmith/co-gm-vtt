Argynvost is a silver dragon who has taken on the guise of a nobleman named Lord Argynvost. He is a valiant protector of good and has rallied knights to his cause against the forces of Strahd. Despite his noble intentions, his efforts ultimately fell short against the power of the vampire lord.

Argynvost is a noble dragon committed to defending his homeland and upholding a legacy of honor. He leads his knights with a sense of duty and valor, determined to confront the evil forces threatening his land, even in his dying moments. His lamentation over his scattered remains reflects a deep connection to his past and a desire for remembrance.

## DM Notes
*Hidden information about Argynvost's true form and objectives can be revealed throughout the campaign.*
