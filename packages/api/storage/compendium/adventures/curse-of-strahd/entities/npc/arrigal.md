Arrigal is a colorfully dressed Vistani who delivers an urgent message to the adventurers, inviting them to Barovia for assistance. He speaks with an accent and carries a strong demeanor, ensuring his message is taken seriously. Despite his charming exterior, he harbors secrets about his true allegiance to the dark lord Strahd.

Arrigal is a dangerous character among the Vistani, with an air of menace that truly sets him apart. He carefully monitors the activities of his people and has little tolerance for weakness or disobedience.

Arrigal is the leader of the Vistani and is tracking Ezmerelda for her perceived crime of horse theft. His methods are calculating, and he maintains a cool demeanor despite his ruthless ambitions.

## DM Notes
Arrigal might reveal his ties to Strahd or other secrets if the characters engage him diplomatically.
