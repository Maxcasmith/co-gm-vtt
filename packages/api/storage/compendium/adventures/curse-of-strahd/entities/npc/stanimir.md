Stanimir is the leader of a camp of Vistani, who entices adventurers with tales of their past and the burdens of their noble prince. He offers hospitality and shares stories, hiding the true purpose of their visit to Barovia and their servitude to Strahd. His calm demeanor masks the dark realities of the land.
