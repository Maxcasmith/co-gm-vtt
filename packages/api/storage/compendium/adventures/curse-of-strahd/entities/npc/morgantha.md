Morgantha is a powerful hag who controls her coven through fear and manipulation. She is ruthless in her pursuit of power and willing to sacrifice to maintain her dominion. Her daughters serve a purpose, but she has no qualms about replacing them with new offspring if necessary.
## DM Notes
Morgantha is deeply tied to Strahd's power, viewing him with both fear and respect.

Morgantha is a cunning night hag operating from Old Bonegrinder. She bakes dream pastries using sinister ingredients, including the bones of children. Her demeanor is both businesslike and menacing, and she fiercely protects her secrets.
