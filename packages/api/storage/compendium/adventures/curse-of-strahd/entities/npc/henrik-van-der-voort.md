Henrik is the owner of the coffin maker's shop, a man who feels the weight of the death around him. He struggles with the demands placed upon him by Strahd, having fallen into the grips of fear and grim necessity.

Henrik van der Voort is the coffin maker whose craftsmanship produces the plain wooden coffin that carries ominous implications. He is known for his quality work, which dwells on the eerie.

## DM Notes
Henrik might be found uneasy about the coffin he made and could hint at the supernatural elements involved.
