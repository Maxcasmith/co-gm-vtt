Madam Eva is a fortune-teller of the Vistani, revered for her ability to foresee the fates that await those who enter Barovia. She uses her deck of tarokka cards to guide the adventurers on their quest against Strahd, representing both a source of hope and danger as her readings reveal paths filled with dark choices.
## DM Notes
Ensure players understand her insights come at a price, possibly leading to unforeseen consequences.
