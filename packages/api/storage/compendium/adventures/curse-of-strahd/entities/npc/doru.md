Doru is the son of the priest Donavich, cursed to roam the night as a mindless undead. His existence is a source of torment for his father and a puzzle for those who seek answers about Strahd's dark influences.

**Doru** is a **vampire spawn** sent by Strahd to torment **Donavich** and cast down the church. He is starved for blood and brave enough to attack a lone character. If the characters approach as a group, he tries to avoid them while hissing, "I can smell your blood!"
