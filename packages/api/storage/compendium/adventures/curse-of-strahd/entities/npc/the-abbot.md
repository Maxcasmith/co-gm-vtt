The Abbot is a mysterious figure who oversees the Abbey of Saint Markovia, rumored to have a sinister connection to Strahd. His charm and persuasive presence have led to his influence over the lost souls within the abbey.

## DM Notes
If approached wisely, The Abbot might reveal more about the abbey's past or the true nature of Strahd.

The Abbot is a mysterious figure who oversees the abbey and its twisted inhabitants. He is determined to create a bride for Strahd and sees himself as a savior of sorts for the land. His misguided intentions often lead him to make horrific decisions, causing tension with the villagers.

The Abbot serves as a powerful figure in Krezk, with the ability to raise the dead. His motivations are intertwined with the fate of the Krezkov family, and he can wield his powers as leverage, putting him in a morally ambiguous position.

The Abbot’s hidden agenda is yet to be revealed, and players may uncover it through interactions.
