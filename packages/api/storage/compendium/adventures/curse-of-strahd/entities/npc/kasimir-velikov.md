Kasimir Velikov is a dusk elf seeking to revive his long-dead sister through dark means. When he finds the dark gift of Zhudun, he enlists the help of the characters to escort him to the catacombs of Castle Ravenloft. His motivations are rooted in love and loss, driving him to take grave risks.
