Kretyana Dolvof is a local midwife who is summoned to assist with childbirth. Her belief in newborns lacking souls if they do not cry adds an intriguing layer to her character and creates tension in the narrative.

## DM Notes
If interacted with, she could share deeply personal worries with characters.
