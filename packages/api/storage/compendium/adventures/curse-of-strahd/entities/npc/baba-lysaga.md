Baba Lysaga is an almost mythic figure who embodies the dark magic of Barovia. Living in the ruins of Berez, she crafts evil magic with a primary focus on hunting down ravens and wereravens. Her obsession with blood rituals showcases her desperation to stave off the effects of old age.

Baba Lysaga is a powerful and ancient hag who resides in a ramshackle hut in Berez. She has a twisted sense of motherhood and uses dark magic to sustain her life.

Baba Lysaga is a malevolent sorceress known for her antagonism towards the Martikov family and their winery. She employs scarecrows as her minions and has designs on acquiring the magical gems that sustain the vineyard's vitality. Her connection to dark magic makes her a formidable presence in Barovia.
