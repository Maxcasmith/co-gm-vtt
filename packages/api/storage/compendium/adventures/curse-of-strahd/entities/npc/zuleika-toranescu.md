Zuleika is a werewolf driven by hatred for Strahd and his minions, particularly her pack leader Kiril. Her vengeance fuels her desire to ally with those opposing the dark powers in Barovia.

Zuleika Toranescu is the mate of Emil, distraught over his fate and resentful of Kiril Stoyanovich's leadership. She sees Kiril's dominance as unjust and wishes to exact revenge for her mate's punishment. Confined to the den, Zuleika's desire for freedom and vengeance may compel her to seek the aid of others.

## DM Notes
Zuleika's conflict with Kiril and her grief over Emil can be compelling for character interactions.

Zuleika Toranescu is a desperate figure praying for her husband Emil's safe return. She sees the players as potential allies in her struggle against Kiril.
