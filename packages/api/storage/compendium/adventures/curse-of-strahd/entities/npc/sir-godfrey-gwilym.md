Sir Godfrey Gwilym is a revenant, lingering as a spirit torn by duty. His past influences his interactions with those who seek to confront Strahd and may inspire courage in those he meets.

Sir Godfrey was a paladin and a prominent figure within the Order of the Silver Dragon. As a revenant, he strives for redemption and has powerful spells at his disposal, but feels bound to the command of Vladimir Horngaard until the beacon is lit.
