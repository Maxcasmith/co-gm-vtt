Donavich is a priest in Barovia who has endured immense hardship due to his son’s transformation into a vampire spawn. His despair drives him to seek a way to end the curse that holds his son captive.

**Donavich** (LG male human **acolyte**) has been praying throughout the night. His voice is hoarse and weak. He is, in a word, insane. A little more than a year ago, his twenty-year-old son **Doru** and several other villagers stormed Castle Ravenloft in revolt. By all accounts, Doru returned to his father as a vampire spawn.

Donavich is a priest who suggests bringing Ireena to Krezk. His motivations and grief over personal loss influence his actions and advice to the players, revealing layers of complexity in his character.

## DM Notes
Donavich may hold insights into the hauntings of the land, drawing on his experiences.
