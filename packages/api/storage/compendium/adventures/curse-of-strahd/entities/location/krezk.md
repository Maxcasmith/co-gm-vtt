West of the two mountains, atop a hill, stands the Abbey of Saint Markovia, around which the Barovians built a walled village named Krezk.

>> The fortified village of Krezk lies at the west end of the valley and is built around an old abbey.
Krezk is known for its strong walls and the protective environment it tries to maintain against the encroachments of Strahd. The villagers are resilient, often banding together to uphold their traditions and safety.

## DM Notes
Krezk can serve as a refuge for players, providing opportunities for quest-giving and local lore.

Krezk is a remote village in Barovia, noted for its connection to the Abbey of St. Markovia. As one of the key locations, it plays a central role in the unfolding events surrounding the Krezkov family and their encounters with supernatural forces.

## DM Notes
Krezk has potential hidden areas and local superstitions that may affect players' explorations.

This nearby village is where characters can take the rescued children. The villagers care for them, providing food and shelter until they can be safely returned to their homes.
