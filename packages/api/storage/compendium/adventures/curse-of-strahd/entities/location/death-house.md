Death House is the name given to an old row house in the village of Barovia (area E7 on the village map). The house has been burned to the ground many times, only to rise from the ashes time and time again—by its own will or that of Strahd. Locals give the building a wide berth for fear of antagonizing the evil spirits believed to haunt it.

>> The mists of Ravenloft continue to surround Death House until the characters stand atop the dais and either appease or defy the cultists.

Death House is a nightmarish abode filled with shifting architecture and traps that threaten the characters' lives. Those who enter must navigate its dangers and face the grim power held within.
