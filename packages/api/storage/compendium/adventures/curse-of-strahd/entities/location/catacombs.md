>> If Strahd prevails, he seals the characters in the catacombs (chapter 4, area K84, crypt 23) and instructs his servants to hide all their magic items.
The catacombs serve as a dark, oppressive prison where Strahd's enemies may meet their end, hidden away from the light and isolated from the world above.
