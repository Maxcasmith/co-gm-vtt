Argynvostholt is the corrupted bastion of the dragon order once devoted to fighting evil. It serves as a relic of heroism that now lies in shadow, where adventurers may uncover hidden legacies or confront their past.

Between Vallaki and Krezk lie the ruins of Argynvostholt, the fallen bastion of a knightly order called the Order of the Silver Dragon, wiped out by Strahd and his army.

West of town is a haunted mansion (see chapter 7, "Argynvostholt"). Legend has it that a dragon died there long, long ago.

>>I was looking at dead men. Before another hour passed, I'd send them wailing on their way to rotting hell. All of them.
>>
>>- **Strahd von Zarovich** in I, Strahd: The Memoirs of a Vampire

Since the dragon's death, Argynvostholt has become a haunted ruin, a former bastion of nobility and light turned into a place of desolation and unrest.

>> The ruins of Argynvostholt stand silent, shrouded in despair and regret. Here, the once-noble knights of the Order of the Silver Dragon now brood over their lost honor, consumed by hatred.

The site is haunted by the spirits of these knights, who are unable to find peace due to their corruption and Vladimir's unrelenting vengefulness.

>>High above the river valley juts a quiet promontory upon which looms a sepulchral mansion, its turrets capped with fairytale cones, its towers lined with sculpted battlements. A third of the structure has collapsed, as has part of the roof, but the rest appears intact. A dark, octagonal tower rises above the surrounding architecture.
>>
>>Out of the fog comes a distant peal of thunder, quickly accompanied by the howling of wolves in the woods below, but the house stands silent, seeming like the fossilized remains of some long-dead thing smote upon the mountainside.

## DM Notes
Hidden areas, traps, secrets, things players haven't discovered yet.

>> This encounter occurs while the characters are inside Argynvostholt.

Argynvostholt is a haunted mansion that may harbor enemies of Strahd, drawing in characters seeking secrets to combat the vampire. It’s a place of dark history and echoes of past tragedies.

>>My one regret is that my remains will not lie in their rightful place, in the hallowed mausoleum of Argynvostholt. No doubt my bones will be scattered among my enemies like the coins of a plundered hoard, trophies of a hard-won victory.

Argynvostholt serves as a solemn resting place for Argynvost and a symbol of his legacy and the Order of the Silver Dragon. It stands as a reminder of valor in the ongoing battle against evil.
