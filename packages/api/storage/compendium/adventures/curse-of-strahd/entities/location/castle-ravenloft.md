>>*Under raging storm clouds, a lone figure stands silhouetted against the ancient walls of Castle Ravenloft. The vampire Count Strahd von Zarovich stares down a sheer cliff at the village below. A cold, bitter wind spins dead leaves about him, billowing his cape in the darkness.*

Castle Ravenloft looms over the land of Barovia, exuding an aura of dread and mystery. Its spires cut through the stormy sky, serving as a fitting abode for the tragic vampire Strahd, who crafts dark plots against those who dare to enter his domain.

>>The village of Barovia cowers in the shadow of Castle Ravenloft, Strahd's home and fortress. The castle stands atop a great spire of rock, invincible and ever watchful. Every night, thousands of bats fly out of the castle to feed. It is said that Strahd sometimes flies with them.

Castle Ravenloft is a place of great dark power, filled with secrets, traps, and the essence of Strahd’s sinister presence. It serves as the final destination for adventurers seeking to confront the vampire lord.
## DM Notes
The interior of the castle is filled with challenges and should evoke a sense of dread and danger.

## Castle Ravenloft

This is the castle where Strahd makes his home, filled with mysteries and dangers.

The campaign is deeply intertwined with the lore surrounding this location.

>>A loud clamor fills the unhallowed halls of Ravenloft. Cries of "Kill the vampire!" are mixed with bold voices shouting, "Never again!" and "To the crypts!"

Angry villagers who have entered the castle brandish torches and pitchforks in a ridiculous display of force. Everywhere they go, they shout for justice. They follow the characters unless prevented from doing so. As long as these Barovians are with the adventurers, random encounters occur on a roll of 9 or higher.

>> The imposing silhouette of Castle Ravenloft looms over the land, a constant reminder of Strahd's power.

This castle serves as the dwelling place of Strahd von Zarovich and a site of confrontation for the undead knights and the Vistani seer Madam Eva.

>>Strahd can't hide his surprise as death takes him into the black abyss. Surprise turns to rage, and the Pillarstone of Ravenloft trembles with fury, shaking dust from the ceiling of the vampire's tomb.

Castle Ravenloft looms as a dark and imposing structure, embodying both the history of Strahd and the nightmares of Barovia.
