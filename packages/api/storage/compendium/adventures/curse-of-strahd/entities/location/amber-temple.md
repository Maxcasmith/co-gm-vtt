>> The Amber Temple is a cold, dark place carved out of the snowy slope of Mount Ghakis. The temperature throughout the complex is −10 degrees Fahrenheit (−23 degrees Celsius). Characters who don't have heat sources, cold weather gear, or magic to protect them are subject to the effects of extreme cold.

The Amber Temple was built to contain malevolent entities and forbidden knowledge. It is a site of significant dark power and the backdrop for many sinister events, housing terrible secrets and a strong presence of evil.

>> The dusk elf comes to the temple on occasion to petition the dark god into releasing his master from his torment. 
The Amber Temple is a dark and mysterious locale where powerful entities intersect. It is a site of both sacrifice and secrets, drawing individuals with various dark intentions.

## DM Notes
The temple holds hidden dangers and ancient powers, making it a critical focal point for character exploration and events.
