## Traits
- **Coven Spellcasting**: Hags possess shared spellcasting abilities as long as they remain in a coven.

## Combat Role
Hags are dangerous foes, especially when working together as a coven, using their spells to control and manipulate opponents. If one hag is defeated, they become significantly less effective.
