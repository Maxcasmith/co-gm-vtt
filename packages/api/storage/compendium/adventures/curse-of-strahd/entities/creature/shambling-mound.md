## Traits
- **Monstrous Nature**: The shambling mound lies beneath refuse in a hidden alcove.

## Combat Role
The shambling mound awakens if the cult's sacrificial ritual is interrupted, attacking the characters.

## Traits
- **Amorphous**: The shambling mound can move through a space as narrow as 1 foot wide without squeezing.
- **Engulf**: The shambling mound can engulf a creature within its space, dealing damage.

## Combat Role
The shambling mound is dangerous in combat due to its ability to engulf characters and deal significant damage with its slam attacks. Its high hit points make it a durable opponent.
