## Traits
- **Druids in Battle**: A druid lurking in area W16 joins the fray, bringing additional threats. If the blights in the surrounding area are defeated, new druids and blights from inside the winery join the battle in later rounds.

## Combat Role
Druids support the blights in battle and utilize spells and abilities to maintain pressure on the players.

## Traits
- **Spellcasting**: The druid can cast spells and control blights.
- **Wild Shape**: The druid can transform into animals if needed.

## Combat Role
The druid casts spells to hinder opponents while directing the blights and may flee if cornered.
