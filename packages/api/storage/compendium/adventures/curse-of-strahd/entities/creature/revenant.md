## Traits
- **Undying Vengeance**: A revenant can remain in its body indefinitely in Barovia and seeks vengeance until fulfilled.

## Combat Role
The revenant is relentless, pursuing its goal of vengeance, and can animate new corpses when defeated, making them dangerous foes in prolonged encounters.

Revenants seek vengeance against those they perceive as threats, attacking relentlessly. If they can’t be stopped or appeased, they will return as persistent enemies until finally laid to rest.
