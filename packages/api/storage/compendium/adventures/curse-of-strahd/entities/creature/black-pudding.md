- **Amorphous**: The black pudding can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
- **Corrosion**: Any nonmagical weapon that hits the black pudding corrodes. After dealing damage, the weapon takes a permanent and cumulative -1 penalty to damage rolls. If its penalty drops to -5, the weapon is destroyed.
