## Traits
- **Keen Smell**: The dire wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

## Combat Role
Dire wolves are fierce predators, attacking in packs to overwhelm their prey. Their powerful jaws can deal substantial damage, making them dangerous foes for unprepared adventurers.

## Traits
- **Pack Tactics**: Has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of its allies is within 5 feet of the creature.

## Combat Role
Dire wolves serve Arrigal as fierce bodyguards and are loyal to Strahd, making them a dangerous threat for any who oppose them.
