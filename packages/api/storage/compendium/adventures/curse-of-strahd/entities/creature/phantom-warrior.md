The phantom warriors serve as protective spirits bound to the remnants of the Order of the Silver Dragon. They emerge from their resting places to defend against intruders.

Phantom warriors are spectral remnants of soldiers bound by duty or loyalty. They possess memories of their past lives but can choose to end their existence at any time. Their incorporeal nature allows them to perform formidable attacks, deflecting attacks with an energy shield.
## Traits
- **Task Driven**: They willingly linger to fulfill their honor or duty.
- **Faded Memories**: Retain recent memories but forget older ones.
## Combat Role
Phantom warriors can deal substantial damage while being able to move through solid objects.
