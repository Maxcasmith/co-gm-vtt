## Traits
- **Chaotic Evil**: Sangzor is a giant goat known for its supernatural resilience and evil disposition.
- **Damage Resistance**: Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.

## Combat Role
The giant goat charges down the mountainside and rams a character. If the attack hits and the target fails its saving throw, it is sent tumbling down the mountainside, falling 100 feet onto a ledge. The goat flees if it takes 10 damage or more.
