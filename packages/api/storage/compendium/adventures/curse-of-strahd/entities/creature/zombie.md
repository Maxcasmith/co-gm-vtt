- **Undead Fortitude**: If damage reduces the zombie to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is from radiant energy or a critical hit. On a success, the zombie drops to 1 hit point instead.

## Traits
- **Undead**: Zombies are mindless creatures that require some control to act.

## Combat Role
Zombies serve as a distracting horde in battle, overwhelming foes with their numbers rather than strength.
