## Traits
- **Fire Breath**: The dragon exhales fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 28 (8d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

## Combat Role
The red dragon wyrmlings are aggressive guardians that attack intruders using their fire breath and claws.
