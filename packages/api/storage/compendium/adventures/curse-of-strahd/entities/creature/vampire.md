## Traits
- **Misty Escape**: When reduced to 0 hit points, the vampire can turn into mist and retreat to its coffin, remaining invulnerable until it recovers.

## Combat Role
In combat, a vampire is a cunning foe, utilizing its charm and strengths in melee combat to weaken and subdue opponents.
