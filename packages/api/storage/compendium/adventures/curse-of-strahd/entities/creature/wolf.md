## Traits
- **Keen Smell**: The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

## Combat Role
Wolves often hunt in packs, coordinating their attacks and using their keen sense of smell to track down prey. Though individually weak, they can become a significant threat when overwhelming characters in numbers.

## Traits
- **Pack Tactics**: The wolf has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Combat Role
Wolves are agile hunters that work in packs to overwhelm their prey, using teamwork to gain an upper hand in combat.
