The wereravens are a group of nine individuals able to transform between human and raven forms. They hide in the grove north of the vineyard and are protective of their home and family.

## Traits
- **A Kindness of Wereravens**: Groups of wereravens are known as kindnesses, typically numbering between seven and twelve individuals.
- **Charitable Collectors**: They enjoy collecting shiny objects and distributing wealth to those in need.

## Combat Role
Wereravens prefer to avoid fighting but can be formidable in their human or hybrid forms, relying on stealth and cunning to achieve their goals.
