Mongrelfolk are a deformed and tragic race resulting from magical experiments. They are driven to steal food but pose no physical threat to villagers if left unprovoked. 

## Traits
- **Stealthy**: They can move quietly, making it difficult for others to spot them until they act.

## Combat Role
Mongrelfolk will typically flee if confronted but are clever enough to use stealth to their advantage in ambush situations.

Mongrelfolk are humanoids transformed by horrific magic, blending various beast features into their forms. They are often outcasts in society, living on the fringes and exhibiting skittish behavior. Despite their grotesque appearances, they are adept at camouflage and can mimic sounds to distract enemies.
## Traits
- **Sound Mimicry**: They can imitate sounds made by beasts and humanoids they've heard.
- **Outcasts**: They are seldom welcomed in humanoid societies and live hidden lives.
- **Camouflage Experts**: They use natural materials to hide their deformities.
## Combat Role
Mongrelfolk use stealth and their ability to mimic sounds to ambush or distract foes.
