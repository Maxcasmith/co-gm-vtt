- **Infectious Spores**: A creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the tree blight must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of its next turn.
- **Multiattack**: The tree blight makes two slam attacks.

The Tree Blight is a dangerous foe, using its terrain to its advantage and able to spread poisonous spores to incapacitate foes.

## Traits
- **Grows from the ground**: The tree blight erupts from the ground, signaling the completion of a druid's ritual. 

## Combat Role
The tree blight is summoned by the druids and acts as a devastating force, capable of laying waste to areas it is ordered to attack.
