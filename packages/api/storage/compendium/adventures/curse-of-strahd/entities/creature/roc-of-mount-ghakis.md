## Traits
- **Unearthly Size**: A creature of unearthly size—a bird so monstrous that its wings blot out the sky.

## Combat Role
The roc attacks a random creature on the bridge, snatching up a horse or a mule, if one is available. Otherwise, it attacks a party member. It can't reach characters who hide in the guard posts at either end of the bridge.
