## Traits
- **Lightning Breath**: The dragon can use its action to exhale lightning in a 5-foot-wide, 30-foot line.

## Combat Role
Young blue dragons are cunning and aggressive, using their innate spellcasting ability to gain advantages in combat. They engage in direct attacks, making the battlefield explosive with their lightning breath.
