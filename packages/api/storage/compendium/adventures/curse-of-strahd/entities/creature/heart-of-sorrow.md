## Traits
- **Connected to Strahd**: Any damage Strahd takes is transferred to the Heart of Sorrow.

## Combat Role
The Heart of Sorrow acts as a guardian of the tower, having the ability to damage intruders while also being vulnerable to their attacks. Destroying the heart disrupts the flow of power within the tower.
