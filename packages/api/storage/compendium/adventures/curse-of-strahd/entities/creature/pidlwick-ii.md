Pidlwick II is a clockwork effigy that embodies the essence of its original, a fool named Pidlwick. Designed as an entertainment piece, it has developed a dark side, resorting to murder to entertain its twisted desires. The effort to replace its predecessor with limited success has led it to become a source of fear rather than joy.
## Traits
- **Evil Toy**: Pidlwick II demonstrates a murderous nature while trying to find companionship.
## Combat Role
It stealthily approaches its 'guests' to incapacitate them without alerting others.
