## Traits
- **Pounce**: If the tiger moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a melee attack on this turn, that target must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

## Combat Role
The saber-toothed tiger is a formidable predator that attacks when agitated. It becomes dangerous and aggressive when wounded, targeting those who inflicted the harm.
