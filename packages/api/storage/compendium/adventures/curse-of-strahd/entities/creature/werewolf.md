## Traits
- **Keen Smell**: Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.
- **Shapechanger**: The werewolf can use its action to polymorph into a wolf-humanoid hybrid or back into its true form, which is a humanoid. Its statistics, other than its AC, remain the same in each form.

## Combat Role
Werewolves are skilled hunters that utilize their speed and ferocity to ambush targets, making them a terror in the misty woods where they thrive. Their ability to shapeshift gives them versatility, allowing for tactical advantages in encounters.

Werewolves are dangerous foes capable of dealing significant damage with their claws and bites while in hybrid form. Their ability to transform allows them to catch opponents off-guard and utilize hit-and-run tactics.

## Traits
- **Hybrid Form**: Werewolves can shift between wolf and human forms at will.  
- **Pack Tactics**: They have advantage on attacks against creatures if at least one of the werewolf's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Combat Role
Werewolves are formidable opponents in combat, utilizing their hybrid forms for both melee and tactical advantages. They work best in packs, coordinating their attacks for maximum effectiveness.
