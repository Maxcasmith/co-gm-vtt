## Traits
- **Crawling**: This zombie is missing both of its legs, so that only its head, torso, and arms remain. It uses its arms to drag itself across the floor.

## Combat Role
The crawling zombie can surprise characters if they are moving quietly and not using light sources. Its attacks are relentless until defeated.
