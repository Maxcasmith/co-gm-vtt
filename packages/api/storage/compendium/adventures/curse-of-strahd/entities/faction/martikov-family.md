The Martikov Family oversees the Wizard of Wines winery and vineyard, dedicated to providing wine to the Barovian people. As werecrows, their efforts are not only a means of survival but also a source of hope for the weary inhabitants of Barovia. They face ongoing threats from the likes of Baba Lysaga and evil druids who seek to undermine their livelihood.
