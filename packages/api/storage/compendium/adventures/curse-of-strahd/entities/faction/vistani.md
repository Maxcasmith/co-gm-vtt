The Vistani are a nomadic people known for their mystique and fortune-telling skills. They travel between worlds in covered wagons and use their abilities to enchant and deceive adventurers that cross into Strahd's domain, often acting as both guides and manipulators.
## DM Notes
Some Vistani may be loyal to Strahd, while others could have their own agendas. Their exact motives may be concealed until later in the story.

The Vistani are a nomadic group with deep ties to the land of Barovia. They serve Strahd, facilitating both entry to and exit from the cursed valley for those who may be tricked or drawn in. Despite outward appearances of freedom and joy, they are bound by ancient debts and dark obligations to Strahd.

The Vistani are a mysterious group of people who have special ties to Strahd, granted freedom to leave Barovia despite the oppressive atmosphere of the land. They hold insight into the realm's secrets and often act as informants or guides for those seeking to navigate its dark paths.

## DM Notes
The Vistani can play a dual role as allies or antagonists, depending on player interactions and intentions.

The Vistani (singular: Vistana) are wanderers, traveling about in horse-drawn, barrel-topped wagons, which they build themselves. Compared to Barovians, they are flamboyant, dressing in bright clothes and laughing often. As much as they feel at home in Strahd's dreary land, they know they can leave it whenever they please and aren't damned to spend eternity there.

## Vistani Bandits

>>You catch a whiff of pipe smoke in the cold air and hear laughter through the fog.

These Vistani **servants of Strahd** march through the Barovian wilderness, laughing and telling ghost stories. They are searching for graves to plunder or hunting small game. For a price of 100 gp, they offer to serve as guides.

The Vistani are a powerful group known for their connections with Strahd and manipulation of events throughout the land. They often serve as spies, informants, and mercenaries, playing both sides in the ongoing struggle.

The Vistani are a nomadic people known for their skills in fortune-telling and gathering secrets. They maintain a delicate balance of power in Barovia, often interacting with various factions and characters for their own survival.

The Vistani are a mysterious people known for their uncanny powers and connection to the supernatural. They are often feared and misunderstood, yet they wield significant influence through their curses and blessings.
