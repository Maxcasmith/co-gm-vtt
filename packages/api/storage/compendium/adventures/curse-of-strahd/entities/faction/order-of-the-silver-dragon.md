The Order of the Silver Dragon is a noble faction dedicated to fighting evil, particularly those dark beings like Strahd that threaten the innocent. Their emblem, a silver dragon, symbolizes hope and courage in the battle against despair.

The Order of the Silver Dragon is a prestigious group of knights who banded together under the leadership of Lord Argynvost. Their mission was to defend the valley from malevolent forces, particularly against the dark lord Strahd. Though they fought valiantly, the power of Strahd ultimately overwhelmed them during the war.

The Order of the Silver Dragon, founded by Argynvost, was once an organization of noble knights dedicated to protecting the innocent from the forces of darkness and defending Barovia from Strahd. Their ideals are now tarnished, and their spirits wander the remnants of Argynvostholt, seeking redemption.
