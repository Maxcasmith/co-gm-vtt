## Traits
- **Undead Fortitude**: If damage reduces the zombie to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw; unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit, it drops to 1 hit point instead.
## Combat Role
Strahd zombies are relentless attackers that close in on characters, using their numbers to create a terrifying encounter. Their rotten stench and ability to withstand lethal damage make them formidable foes.
