## Traits
- **Regeneration**: These minions of Strahd can heal unless affected by radiant damage.

## Combat Role
Vampire spawn are rapid attackers who ambush adventurers with swift aggression, swiftly jumping down from above.

## Traits
- **Spider Climb**: Vampire Spawn can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

## Combat Role
Vampire Spawn are relentless predators sent by Strahd to hunt down intruders. They are stealthy and can ambush effectively.
