## Traits
- **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day): If Strahd fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
- **Shapechanger**: Strahd can use his action to polymorph into a Medium wolf or back into his true form.
- **Vampiric Mist**: When reduced to 0 hit points, Strahd can dissipate into mist and reform later at his lair.

## Combat Role
In combat, Strahd utilizes his vampire abilities to charm and dominate his opponents while displaying exceptional agility and cunning. His legendary status enables him to survive lethal encounters and reappear to continue his game of manipulation.

## Traits
- **Regeneration**: Strahd regains hit points at the start of his turn unless he takes radiant damage.

## Combat Role
As a powerful vampire lord, Strahd’s presence looms large; his connections to the Martikovs can drastically alter the stakes of the encounter.
