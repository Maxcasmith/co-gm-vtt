## Traits
- **Magic Resistance**: The night hag has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
- **Dream Haunting**: She can enter the dreams of mortals, inflicting fear and granting a temporary escape.
## Combat Role
Night hags are dangerous foes, using their magical abilities and physicality to exploit the weaknesses of their enemies. Their capability to manipulate dreams makes them particularly treacherous in a campaign setting.
