## Traits
- **Amorphous**: The shambling mound can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
- **Lightning Absorption**: Whenever the shambling mound is subjected to lightning damage, it takes none and regains hit points equal to the damage dealt.

## Combat Role
The shambling mound uses its abilities to ambush and overwhelm characters in close quarters, absorbing damage and regenerating from lightning attacks. It can deal significant damage through its melee attacks, posing a considerable threat in confined spaces.
