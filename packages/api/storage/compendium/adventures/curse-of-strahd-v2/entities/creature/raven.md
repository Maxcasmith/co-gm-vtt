## Traits
- **Keen Sight**: The raven has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.
- **Pack Tactics**: The raven has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the raven's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Combat Role
The raven is quick and agile, often using its keen senses to communicate danger or alert allies. In combat, it can use its numbers to distract foes.
