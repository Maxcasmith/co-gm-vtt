## Traits
- **Pack Tactics**: The swarms have advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the swarm's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

## Combat Role
In the dim light of their alcoves, the swarms mask their true numbers. When threatened, they leap from their hidden positions to swarm and bite.
