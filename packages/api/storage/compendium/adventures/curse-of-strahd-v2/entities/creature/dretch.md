## Traits
- **Innate Spellcasting**: The dretch can cast the *fog cloud* spell once per day each.

## Combat Role
Dretches are low-level demons that act as minions and can create chaos through their innate spellcasting. When summoned by Morgantha, they serve her directly and must be dealt with quickly to prevent them from overwhelming the party.
