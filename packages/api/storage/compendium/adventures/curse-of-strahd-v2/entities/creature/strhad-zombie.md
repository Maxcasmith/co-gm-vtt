## Traits
- **Undead Fortitude**: If damage reduces the zombie to 0 hit points, it can make a saving throw; on a success, it drops to 1 hp instead.

## Combat Role
Strahd zombies maintain relentless attacks, overpowering foes through sheer numbers and resilience.

As reanimated corpses under Strahd's control, Strahd zombies surge forward in combat, fueled by their lingering malice and a relentless desire to ensnare the living.
