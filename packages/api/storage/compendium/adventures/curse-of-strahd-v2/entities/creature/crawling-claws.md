## Traits
- **Mob Rule**: A mob of severed hands, their mummified flesh black with soot, skitters out of the darkness across the dusty floor. They gang up on one party member.

## Combat Role
Crawling claws use stealth and deception in their attack, attempting to hide in backpacks and surprise characters when they are most vulnerable.
