Werewolves are strong, agile creatures cursed to switch between human and wolf forms. They are ferocious in combat, capable of ripping apart foes with their claws. Their curse is often rooted in tragic stories of lost humanity, making them both fearsome adversaries and potential allies.

## Traits
- **Description**: A deep voice calls out, "Who goes there?"

**Werewolves** in human form pretend to be trappers.
