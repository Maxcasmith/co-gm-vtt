Wintersplinter is an enormous tree blight summoned to wreak havoc upon the vineyard, showcasing immense power in its tracks. This creature's presence is felt heavily as it tramples the grapevines, resulting in destruction. When encountered, Wintersplinter fights aggressively to defend its territory and is relentless in its pursuit of destruction.
