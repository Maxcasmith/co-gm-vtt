## Traits
- **Swarm**: They can occupy the same space as another creature and move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
## Combat Role
Swarms of rats are dangerous in numbers, overwhelming opponents with bites and distractions. They are often found in abandoned homes, adding a layer of horror to the exploration.

## Traits
- **Swarm**: The rats act as a swarm, allowing them to attack multiple creatures at once.

## Combat Role
The swarm attacks intruders when disturbed, overwhelming characters with numbers through quick movement and biting.
