## Traits
- **Description**: You see a dark figure crouched low and perfectly still, aiming a crossbow in your direction.

These **scouts** are Barovian hunters or trappers searching for a missing villager or townsperson. Once they realize the characters aren't out to kill them, they lower their weapons and request help in finding their missing person.
