## Traits
- **Description**: A snarling wolf the size of a grizzly bear steps out of the fog.

The **dire wolves** of Barovia are cruel, overgrown wolves and Strahd's loyal servants. They can't be charmed or frightened.

## Traits
- **Keen Hearing and Smell**: The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.
## Combat Role
Dire wolves are stealthy hunters, charging into battle and attempting to corner prey with their pack, making them a dangerous opponent.
