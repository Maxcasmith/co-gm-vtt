## Traits
- **Immutable Form**: The gargoyle is immune to any effect that would alter its form.
- **Stone Form**: The gargoyle can petrify itself as a bonus action, becoming indistinguishable from stone.

## Combat Role
Gargoyles are often used as sentinels and are dangerous foes in combat due to their ability to blend into stone architecture. They can swoop down on unsuspecting characters and utilize their natural weaponry effectively.
