## Traits
- **Smother**: The rug attacks creatures, other than undead, that move across it or anyone who tries to disturb it, attempting to suffocate them.

## Combat Role
The Rug of Smothering is dangerous as it can ensnare and suffocate its victims, making it a stealthy but lethal trap for unwary adventurers.
