## Traits
- **Flight**: The swarm can fly and typically surrounds enemies from above.

## Combat Role
The swarms of ravens perch on the rafters but don't attack the characters under any circumstances. However, if the characters attack the druid, the swarms descend from the rafters and begin attacking the twig blights.
