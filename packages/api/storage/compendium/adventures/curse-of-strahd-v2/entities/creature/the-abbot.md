## Traits
- **Angel in Disguise**: The Abbot is a deva that has lived for millennia. He typically assumes the form of a strikingly handsome human priest in his late twenties or early thirties.
- **Imperfections**: The Abbot's fall from grace started when the Belviews—a family of sickly, inbred lepers—came to the abbey seeking salvation.
- **Bride of Strahd**: Strahd confided in The Abbot, lamenting his curse and telling the deva that he wished nothing more than to escape Barovia.

## Combat Role
The Abbot fights using his magical abilities and seeks to manipulate others to achieve his goals, making him dangerous in encounters.
