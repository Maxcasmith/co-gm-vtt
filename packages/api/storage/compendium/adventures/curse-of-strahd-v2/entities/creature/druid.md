## Traits
- **Description**: A gaunt figure with wild hair and bare feet bounds toward you on all fours.

The Barovian wilderness is home to **druids** who worship Strahd.

## Traits
- **Spellcasting**: The druid can prepare and cast spells as a 5th-level spellcaster.

## Combat Role
Depending on the situation, the druid can attack with magic or summon more twig blights to fight, making him a dangerous foe.
