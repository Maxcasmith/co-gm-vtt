## Traits
- **Commoners**: They usually lack combat skills but are resilient in their own right, often preferred to flee rather than fight.
## Combat Role
Barovian villagers are primarily non-combatants, cowering in fear during encounters. Their role is to provide emotional weight to the setting, reflecting the desolation stemming from Strahd's reign.
