## Traits
- **Size**: A bird so monstrous that its wings blot out the sky.

## Combat Role
The roc attacks a random creature on the bridge, snatching up a horse or a mule. If it has nothing to attack on its turn, the roc lets loose a horrible shriek and flies back to its nest.
