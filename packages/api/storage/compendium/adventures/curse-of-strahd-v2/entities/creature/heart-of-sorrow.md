- **Damage Transfer**: Any damage Strahd takes is transferred to the Heart of Sorrow.

## Combat Role
The Heart of Sorrow acts as a powerful anchor to Strahd's existence, capable of devastating consequences if destroyed. Its pulsating light and the animated defenses make it a formidable target.
