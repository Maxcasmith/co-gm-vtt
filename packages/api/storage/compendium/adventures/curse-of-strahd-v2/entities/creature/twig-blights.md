## Traits
- **Ambusher**: Gains advantage on attack rolls against creatures that haven’t taken a turn in combat.

## Combat Role
There are twenty-four twig blights hiding in the cask, ready to emerge and attack when commanded by their druid master. They remain protected while inside the cask.
