## Traits
- **Description**: A figure walks alone with the stride and bearing of one who knows no fear.

The revenant was a **knight of the Order** of the Silver Dragon, which was annihilated defending the valley against Strahd's armies more than four centuries ago.

## Traits
- **Vengeful Spirit**: A revenant has a year to achieve its revenge before its body crumbles to dust and its soul enters the afterlife.

## Combat Role
In Barovia, revenants can remain in their bodies indefinitely and seek vengeance on those who wronged them. They are relentless attackers and will pursue their targets even across vast distances, making them dangerous foes.
