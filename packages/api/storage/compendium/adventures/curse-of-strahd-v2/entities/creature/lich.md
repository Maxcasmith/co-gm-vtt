## Traits
- **Magic Resistance**: The lich has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
- **Undead Fortitude**: If damage reduces the lich to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit.

## Combat Role
Liches are powerful spellcasters that can manipulate and control undead, making them dangerous foes. They often fight from a distance using devastating spells while their undead minions engage opponents.
