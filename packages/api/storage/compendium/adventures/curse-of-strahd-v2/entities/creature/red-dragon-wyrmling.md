## Traits
- **Fire Breath**: The wyrmling can exhale fire in a 15-foot cone as an action, dealing damage in an area.

## Combat Role
Red dragon wyrmlings are fierce combatants with powerful breath attacks. They can take to the air and attack intruders, giving them a tactical advantage.
