## Traits
- **Immutable Form**: The golem is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

## Combat Role
Clay Golems are sturdy defenders that can withstand considerable punishment. They will primarily engage in melee combat, utilizing their powerful strikes against foes who threaten their territory.
