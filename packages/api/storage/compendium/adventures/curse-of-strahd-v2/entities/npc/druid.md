The druid is a guardian of the vineyard, using their powers to command the blights. With a secret weapon, the Gulthias staff, they pose a significant threat to intruders trying to breach the winery's defenses.

A wild-looking figure with a gown made of animal skins and a headdress with goat horns, she pours syrup into fermentation vats to poison the wine. She commands the twig blights and will attack if threatened.
