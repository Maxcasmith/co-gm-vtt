Madam Eva is a mysterious fortune-teller who uses a deck of tarokka cards to predict the fates of the adventurers in Barovia. She serves as a guide, leading them on a dark course through the land ruled by Strahd, helping them uncover the secrets of their future. As a Vistani, her people are known for their manipulative and elusive nature, allowing her to draw adventurers further into the deadly games of Strahd. 
## DM Notes
Madam Eva's knowledge of the land and Strahd's secrets can be revealed at crucial moments, enhancing the tension of the adventure.

Madam Eva is an old Vistani seer who can perform card readings to discern the fortunes of those who seek her guidance. She is wise and shrewd, often speaking in riddles and metaphors that hint at deeper truths about her clients’ destinies.
