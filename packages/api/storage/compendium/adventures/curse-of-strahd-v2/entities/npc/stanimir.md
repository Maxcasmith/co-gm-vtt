Stanimir, the leader of a group of Vistani, welcomes the characters to his camp and offers them an enticing story, hoping to recruit them for his cause. His demeanor is warm, yet his allegiance to Strahd complicates his true intentions.

## DM Notes
Stanimir's storytelling has deeper implications and can reveal more about the curse on Strahd.
