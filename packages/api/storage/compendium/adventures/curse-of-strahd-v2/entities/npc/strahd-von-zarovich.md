Strahd von Zarovich is a powerful vampire and the master of Castle Ravenloft, haunted by a tragic past that has twisted him into a monster. With a commanding presence, he embodies both allure and menace, showing a profound understanding of the dark romanticism that surrounds his existence. As adventurers enter his domain, they become pawns in his intricate schemes, revealing his meticulous and predatory nature.
## DM Notes
Strahd is aware of the adventurers’ intentions and is plotting each step of their encounter.

Strahd von Zarovich is the dark lord of Barovia, a powerful vampire and wizard who exerts control over the land and its inhabitants. His pursuit of Ireena Kolyana stems from a deep-seated obsession, complicating his role as a tormentor and hypnotic figure in the lives of adventurers who enter his domain. He strives to manipulate events to his advantage, often turning friends against each other to showcase his cunning. 
## DM Notes
Strahd watches the adventurers closely once he becomes aware of them. He invites them to his castle, where he plans to torment them, using his powers and resources.
