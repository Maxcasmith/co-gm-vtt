Arrigal is a colorfully dressed Vistani who approaches the characters in a tavern, delivering a message from his master. He appears confident yet secretive, urging the adventurers to aid his master, Strahd, while pretending to serve the local burgomaster. After delivering his message, he mounts his horse and departs.

## DM Notes
Arrigal is a deceptive character whose actions are ultimately aligned with Strahd's plans.

Arrigal is one of the brothers running the Vistani camp and is more cunning than his brother Luvash. He serves Strahd's interests tightly, often using his skills in stealth to distract foes or eliminate potential problems for his faction.
