Doru is the son of Donavich, who longs for the light of his former life while battling the dark urges of his vampiric nature. His character embodies tragedy and the struggle between familial love and the curse of darkness that consumes him.

Doru is the son of Donavich, transformed into a vampire spawn due to a failed revolt against Strahd. His cries for help echo from the church's undercroft, where he remains trapped, starved for blood and yearning for release from his cursed existence.
