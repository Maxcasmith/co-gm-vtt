Rudolph van Richten is a renowned monster hunter and scholar who has focused his life on defeating the evil Strahd von Zarovich. He is deeply invested in his research but has made sacrifices that include destroying his work to protect crucial information. His hubris has made him elusive, and his past shadows his current intentions.
## DM Notes
Information about van Richten and his notes are critical for plot development, specifically for players seeking to understand Strahd's weaknesses.
