Helga Ruvak is a deceptive vampire spawn, feigning innocence as she dusts the room and hums. Although she claims to have been kidnapped and forced into service by Strahd, her agenda is far murkier, as she waits for the opportunity to betray any who might show trust. A ruby pendant around her neck, a token from Strahd, highlights her dark allegiance.
