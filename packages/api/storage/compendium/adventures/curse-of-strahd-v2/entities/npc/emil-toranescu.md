Emil Toranescu is a young man with a grim fate; he is a werewolf who has found himself ensnared in Strahd's dungeons. Inquisitive and desperate, he seeks help from adventurers while grappling with the consequences of his past actions.

Emil Toranescu is a pivotal figure in the werewolf den, capable of commanding the werewolves not to attack intruders when he is present. His presence is crucial for the characters seeking safe passage through the den.
