Ireena Kolyana is a striking woman who resembles the long-lost Tatyana, caught in a tragic love triangle involving Strahd. Her determination to understand her past makes her a key figure in the ongoing conflict.
## DM Notes
Strahd is obsessed with Ireena, believing her to contain the spirit of his lost love, heightening the danger she faces.
