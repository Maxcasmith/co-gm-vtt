Sergei was the younger brother of Strahd von Zarovich and was cherished by their mother, Ravenovia. His life was cut tragically short by Strahd, who envied Sergei's relationship with their mother and coveted Tatyana's love, leading to betrayal and murder. Sergei’s death solidified Strahd’s dark transformation into a vampire and painted the horrific path that followed in Barovia.
## DM Notes
Sergei's death is a crucial part of Strahd's motivation and backstory.

Sergei is a figure of great importance to Ireena and embodies the hope of love transcending death. His presence adds complexity to the narrative and highlights the stakes involved in the characters' journey.
