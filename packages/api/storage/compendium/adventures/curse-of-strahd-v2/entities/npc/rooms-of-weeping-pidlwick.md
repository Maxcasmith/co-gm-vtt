Pidlwick is a ghostly jester who appears when summoned by playing the harp. He carries a whimsical demeanor but offers valuable information about hidden treasures.
## DM Notes
Pidlwick's tragic fall adds a layer of humor to his character, creating a mix of lightheartedness and gloom in the gloomy context of Ravenloft.
