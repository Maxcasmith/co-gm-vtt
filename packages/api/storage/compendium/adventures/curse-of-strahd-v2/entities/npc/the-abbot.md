The Abbot presides over the Abbey of Saint Markovia and is a figure wrapped in both reverence and fear. Many villagers suspect he may be Strahd in disguise, adding to the air of mystery surrounding his role.

The Abbot is a powerful and enigmatic figure who aids the residents of Krezk, yet his methods can be manipulative. He is committed to restoring life and sees the Krezkov bloodline as essential. His willingness to resurrect Ilya Krezkov reveals both his gifts and his ulterior motives.
