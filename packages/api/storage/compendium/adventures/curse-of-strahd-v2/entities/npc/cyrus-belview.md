Cyrus Belview serves Strahd faithfully within Castle Ravenloft. His loyalty is unquestionable, and he has access to secrets that few others do, including the knowledge of command words that can control various mechanisms throughout the castle.

Cyrus Belview is a mongrelfolk and Strahd's faithful servant. He is a hunchbacked figure with various animal features, including lizard scales and a duck's webbed foot. Cyrus is crazy but loyal, often leading characters into traps or revealing secrets unintentionally.
