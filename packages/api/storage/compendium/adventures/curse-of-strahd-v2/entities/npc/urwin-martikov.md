Urwin Martikov is the innkeeper of the Blue Water Inn in Vallaki. He is a jovial and welcoming figure who strives to create a safe environment for his guests. He is also a member of the secret society known as the Keepers of the Feather, meant to oppose the dark powers of Strahd.
