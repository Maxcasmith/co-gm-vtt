Patrina Velikovna is a female dusk elf archmage who was resurrected by her brother, Kasimir. However, despite initially feigning repentance, she has her own ambitions to return to Strahd, seeking to become his vampire bride. Her desire for power drives her actions, and she harbors resentment toward Rahadin for his role in her brother's mutilation.

Patrina Velikovna is a vampire spawn with ambitions of acquiring lost arcane secrets. Following Strahd's death, she begins to plunder knowledge from Castle Ravenloft, determined to become the new ruler of Barovia. Her ambition poses a new threat to the land, making her a potential antagonist for the players to confront. 
## DM Notes
Patrina's ambitions and motivations reflect the ongoing struggle for power in Barovia.
