Morgantha is a night hag disguised as an old woman, peddling dream pastries throughout the village. She uses her guise to manipulate the villagers into parting with their children, believing that her pastries offer some form of escape from their grim reality. Morgantha is cunning and knows how to navigate the fears and desires of those around her.
## DM Notes
Should players confront her, they may learn more about Strahd and the hidden dangers lurking in Barovia.

Morgantha is a powerful hag who leads her coven with a strict and ambitious demeanor. She tolerates her daughters for their utility in maintaining their combined magic but harbors dark intentions should any of them perish.
## DM Notes
Morgantha uses her hag eye to spy on Castle Ravenloft, motivated by her fear of Strahd.
