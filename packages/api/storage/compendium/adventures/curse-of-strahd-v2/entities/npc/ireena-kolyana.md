Ireena Kolyana is the adopted daughter of the burgomaster of the Village of Barovia and is deeply troubled by Strahd's desire for her. The villagers avoid helping her, fearing the vampire's wrath, leaving her to navigate her fate alone. Her uncanny resemblance to Tatyana adds to the mystery surrounding her existence and her importance in Strahd's dark plans. 
## DM Notes
Ireena’s fate is critical to the adventure, and her interactions with the adventurers can lead to various outcomes.

Ireena Kolyana, a young woman of striking beauty, has caught the attention of Strahd von Zarovich, who believes she is the reincarnation of his lost love, Tatyana. Living in the village of Barovia, she is under continuous threat from Strahd's advances, unaware of the dark fate that awaits her as she becomes the target of his obsession. Her desire for a normal life contrasts starkly with the grim destiny prepared for her by the vampire lord.
