Dmitri Krezkov is the burgomaster of the fortified village of Krezk, tasked with protecting its inhabitants and maintaining their stronghold against Strahd's forces. He is vigilant and pragmatic, often considering both the immediate needs and future dangers of Barovia. Dmitri is well-respected and known for his courageous stand against evil.
## DM Notes
To gain his trust, players may need to prove their worthiness against threats from Strahd.
