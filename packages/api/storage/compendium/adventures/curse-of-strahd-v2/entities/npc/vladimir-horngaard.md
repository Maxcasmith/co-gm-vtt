Vladimir Horngaard is the ghostly guardian of Argynvostholt, a former paladin dedicated to combating Strahd. He is consumed by regret and the desire for vengeance, yet still demonstrates a sense of honor and camaraderie towards those who share his goals.

Vladimir Horngaard is the greatest of the knights of the Order of the Silver Dragon. After the death of Argynvost, he returned as a revenant, driven by a singular goal: to avenge his fallen comrades and destroy Strahd. As he leads the corrupted spirits of his fellow knights, his journey poses the question of whether he can find redemption from the hatred that consumes him.
