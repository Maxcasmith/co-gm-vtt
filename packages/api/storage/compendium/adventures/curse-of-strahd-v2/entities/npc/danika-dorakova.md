Danika Dorakova is Urwin's wife and the business partner at the Blue Water Inn. She is warm and welcoming, embodying the spirit of community as she serves the patrons. Like her husband, she possesses abilities that are secretive in nature, enabling her to assist in their fight against the darkness of Barovia.
