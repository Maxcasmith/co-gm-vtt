Tatyana is the embodiment of beauty and youth in Strahd’s heart, igniting his deep-rooted feelings of love and desire. However, her love belongs to Sergei, leading to tragedy as Strahd's jealousy drives him to commit murder. Tatyana's despair and the actions that lead to her falling from Ravenloft create a significant mark on Strahd's story, as her fate remains a mystery that haunts him.

## DM Notes
Tatyana's connection to Strahd and Sergei can serve as an essential narrative thread that players can interact with, potentially unlocking secret histories as they investigate the past.
