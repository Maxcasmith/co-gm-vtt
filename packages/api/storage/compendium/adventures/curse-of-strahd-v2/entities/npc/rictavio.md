Rictavio is a charismatic man known for his tales and the companionship of a monkey. While he seems to be a mere entertainer at the Blue Water Inn, he conceals a powerful past and a connection to the ongoing fight against Strahd.

Rictavio is a charismatic half-elf bard with a knack for storytelling and adventure. He is determined to navigate the dangers of Barovia while maintaining a sense of purpose. His relations with others are often rife with tension, especially with those he has feelings of betrayal against, such as Yan.
