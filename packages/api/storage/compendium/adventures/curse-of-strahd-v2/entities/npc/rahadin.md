Rahadin is Strahd's mysterious chamberlain who often guides intruders in Castle Ravenloft. He appears quiet and ominous, capable of directing the characters if they ask for guidance, but will fight to the death if attacked.

An elf with brown skin and long black hair descends the wide staircase, quiet as a cat. He wears a gray cloak over black studded leather armor and has a polished scimitar hanging from his belt. "My master is expecting you," he says. Rahadin fights only if attacked; otherwise, he leads the characters to the dining hall.
