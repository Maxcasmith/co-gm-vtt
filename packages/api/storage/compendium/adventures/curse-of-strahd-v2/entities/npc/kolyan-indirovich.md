Kolyan Indirovich is the Burgomaster of the Village of Barovia, known for his dedication to his people's welfare. Though he strives to maintain their safety against the oppressive rule of Strahd, he is ultimately limited by Strahd's power and influence.

## DM Notes
Kolyan's letters and messages to the adventurers may be traps set by Strahd.

Kolyan Indirovich is the burgomaster of the village of Barovia, a role that weighs heavily on him under the shadow of Strahd. He is determined to maintain the safety and tradition of his people despite the looming threat of the vampire lord. His leadership is crucial as he navigates the dangers that come with Strahd's reign.
