Baron Vargas Vallakovich is the ambitious burgomaster of Vallaki, known for his strict governance and attempts to bring some sense of normalcy to the chaotic realm. He seeks to maintain control over his town through public celebrations and laws but is deeply fearful of the power Strahd holds over the land. His character blends desperation with an overbearing desire for order.
## DM Notes
Players may need to navigate his complex personality and the tensions between him and his townspeople.

Baron Vargas Vallakovich is the deluded burgomaster of Vallaki, convinced that his festive undertakings will shield the town from Strahd's grasp. He focuses on superficial happiness while overlooking the true threat looming over the citizens.
