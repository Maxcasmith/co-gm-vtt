Lydia Petrovna is Baron Vargas Vallakovich's wife, desperately trying to embody her husband's ideals of happiness. She has become somewhat disillusioned but keeps her spirits up by hosting tea parties for the less fortunate, hoping to distract herself from her family's troubled dynamics.
