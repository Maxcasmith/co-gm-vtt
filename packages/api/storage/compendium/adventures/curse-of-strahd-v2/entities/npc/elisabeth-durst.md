Elisabeth Durst is the mother of Rosavalda and Thornboldt. She was complicit in the cruel treatment of her children by locking them away, believing herself to be acting for their safety.

Elisabeth Durst, the matriarch, shared her husband's dark ambitions. Driven by a desperate need to protect her family and legacy, she became complicit in horrific events that unfolded within the walls of Death House, forever entwining their fates with the ghosts of their past.
