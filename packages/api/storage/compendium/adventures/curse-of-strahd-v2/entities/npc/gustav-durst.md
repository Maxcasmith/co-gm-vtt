Gustav Durst is the father of Rosavalda and Thornboldt. He is a deluded figure who, along with his wife, locked their children in the attic, leading to their tragic demise.

Gustav Durst, the patriarch of the Durst family, is obsessed with his family's reputation and legacy. His desperation to maintain control ultimately led him down a dark path, aligning himself with malevolent forces that have cursed his household and brought about its downfall.
