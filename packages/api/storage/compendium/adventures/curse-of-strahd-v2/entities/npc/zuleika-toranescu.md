Zuleika Toranescu, a werewolf in human form, kneels in prayer at the shrine of Mother Night. Her desperate hope for her husband's safety leads her to seek allies among those who enter the den.

Zuleika is a loyal yet conflicted member of the werewolf pack, torn between her love for Kiril and the safety of her children. As Kiril's possible successor, she must navigate the chaos of leadership and rivalries while seeking to protect her family from harm. Her influence can sway the pack's decisions, especially regarding the kidnapped children.
