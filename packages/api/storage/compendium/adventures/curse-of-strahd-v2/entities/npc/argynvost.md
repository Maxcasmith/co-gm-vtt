Argynvost is a silver dragon who took on human form as Lord Argynvost to lead the fight against evil in the valley. He is noble-hearted and courageous, gathering knights to his order to defend the land. His death at the hands of Strahd's forces marked the end of an era of hope for many who believed in the fight against darkness.

Argynvost is a noble dragon dedicated to defending his home and standing against evil forces. He has witnessed the fall of his knights and the despair enveloping the land but remains steadfast in his commitment to protect the ideals he cherishes. With a fierce determination, he prepares to face his end, hoping that his spirit will serve as a beacon of hope against the darkness.
