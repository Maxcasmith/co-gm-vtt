He often lures adventurers into traps, using both stealth and brute strength to ensure the safety of his pack.

Kiril Stoyanovich is the alpha of the werewolf pack, commanding loyalty and respect. He harbors a sense of authority that belies his vulnerabilities, making him a complex antagonist in the den.
