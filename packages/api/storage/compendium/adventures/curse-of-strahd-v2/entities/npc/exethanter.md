Exethanter is an evil archmage who became a lich after breaching the wards of the Amber Temple. Once a formidable wizard, he has deteriorated over the years, becoming weak and forgetful. Despite his current state, he still holds immense power and knowledge of dark magic within the temple.

Exethanter is a decrepit lich who has lost its memory and a portion of its power. It offers knowledge to those who restore its memories and offers to escort them through the temple, assuming they seek knowledge and power.
