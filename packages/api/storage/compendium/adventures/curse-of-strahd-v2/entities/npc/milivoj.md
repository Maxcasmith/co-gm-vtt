Milivoj is a brawny gravedigger with a furrowed brow, often seen with his shovel. He wrestles with his frustrations regarding the oppressive conditions in Vallaki and his inability to protect his younger siblings. Despite his tough appearance, he feels trapped and desires a better life far from Barovia.
