Ismark Kolyanovich, known as Ismark the Lesser, is the son of the burgomaster. He is friendly and seeks aid from adventurers to protect his sister Ireena from Strahd. Despite his royal lineage, Ismark feels overshadowed by his father's legacy and is determined to confront Strahd directly.
