Lief Lipsiege is a surly accountant pressed into service by Strahd many years ago. Chained to his desk, he records Strahd's riches and maintains the accounts, all the while resenting the vampire for withholding knowledge of other treasures. Despite his grumpiness, he possesses valuable information about hidden treasures within Ravenloft.
