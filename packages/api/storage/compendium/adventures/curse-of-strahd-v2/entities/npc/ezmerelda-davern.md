Ezmerelda is stealthily exploring Castle Ravenloft with the goal of hunting Strahd. She is cautious and assesses the adventurers’ knowledge about vampires before agreeing to join them. If not invited to accompany, she wishes them well and goes on her way.

Ezmerelda is a resourceful and determined character, often seen as a seeker of knowledge. She operates out of her magical wagon, where she keeps various mystical items and secrets. Her motivation lies in uncovering the fate of her mentor, van Richten.
## DM Notes
Ezmerelda has a hidden page of van Richten's journal, which may provide clues for players.
