Davian Martikov is the patriarch of the Martikov family, tasked with ensuring the longevity of the Wizard of Wines. He is a wereraven who is deeply troubled by the recent misfortunes that have befallen his winery, especially the theft of magic gems that sustain their wine production. Frustrated with his son Urwin, he is determined to reclaim the lost gems and restore hope to the Barovian people.

Davian Martikov is the current owner of the winery and vineyard, an old and suspicious man. Until he trusts the characters, he remains tight-lipped about the stolen gems but informs them of recent attacks by evil druids and blights.
