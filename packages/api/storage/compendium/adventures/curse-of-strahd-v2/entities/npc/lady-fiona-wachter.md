Lady Fiona Wachter is a significant figure in Vallaki who harbors hatred for Baron Vargas Vallakovich. She believes that the burgomaster's rule is mad and often expresses a willingness to side with darker powers.

Lady Fiona Wachter is a shrewd and calculating figure with a particular loyalty to Strahd. Obsessed with gaining power, she has initiated a cult within her home while manipulating events in Vallaki to her advantage, aiming to dethrone Baron Vallakovich.
