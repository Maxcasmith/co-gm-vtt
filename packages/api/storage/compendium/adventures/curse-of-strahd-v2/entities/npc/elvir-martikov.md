Elvir is the youngest son of Davian Martikov, part of the family running the vineyard.

Elvir Martikov is another son in the Martikov family, heavily involved in the efforts to secure their winery and support the family. He helps with the physical tasks, such as securing the horses, and often talks about the bond of family during hardships. Elvir's bravery is often on display, but internally, he struggles with the fears associated with the threats around them.
