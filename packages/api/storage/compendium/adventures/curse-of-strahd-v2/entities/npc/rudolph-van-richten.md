Rudolph van Richten is a renowned vampire hunter who has dedicated his life to eradicating the undead and other supernatural evils. After Strahd's fall, he contemplates solitude, highlighting his internal struggles and the toll that his life of battle has taken on him. He serves as a mentor figure, encouraging others to face the challenges ahead. 
## DM Notes
Rudolph's experience adds depth to the narrative, sharing his knowledge with the players.

Rudolph van Richten is a famed and feared hunter of the unnatural, driven by a personal vendetta against the darkness that claimed his child, Erasmus. Despite his reputation as a hero, he views his life as a bleak existence haunted by the consequences of his obsession. The realization that he is under a Vistani curse haunts him as he comes to grips with the tragedies that befall those around him.
