Khazan was an archwizard who played a pivotal role in the construction of Castle Ravenloft. After becoming a lich, he sought to unseat Strahd and become the ruler of Barovia but ended up serving as Strahd's advisor. His ambition led to his downfall, resulting in his self-destruction.

## DM Notes
Khazan's remains lie in the catacombs of Ravenloft, and his history with Strahd can serve as a cautionary tale for players.

Khazan is a powerful spellcaster who has taken extreme measures to protect his domain. He has warded his tower so that only he can cast spells within it, making it difficult for potential intruders to challenge him effectively. This ward manifests as an antimagic field, allowing him to manipulate the very rules that govern magic.
