>>Barovia is a once-idyllic valley, now a prison within a demiplane ruled by Strahd. Surrounded by an impenetrable fog, it is a land of beauty turned dark, plagued by fear, and inhabited by remnants of its former inhabitants who seek freedom from Strahd's tyranny.

## DM Notes
The land itself is part of Strahd's consciousness, and adventurers must navigate its dangers carefully.

Rolling thunderclouds cast a gray pall over the land of Barovia. A deathly stillness hangs over the dark woods, which are patrolled constantly by Strahd's wolves and other servitors.
