>>*Under raging storm clouds, a lone figure stands silhouetted against the ancient walls of Castle Ravenloft. The vampire Count Strahd von Zarovich stares down a sheer cliff at the village below. A cold, bitter wind spins dead leaves about him, billowing his cape in the darkness.*

Castle Ravenloft looms as a dark and foreboding structure, its spires piercing the stormy sky. The castle serves as the home of Strahd and a crucial location for the unfolding horror within the land of Barovia.

>>Castle Ravenloft, Strahd's home and fortress, stands atop a great spire of rock, invincible and ever watchful.

The castle is a place of dark power and many secrets, where adventurers must tread carefully if they hope to confront Strahd. The foreboding structure looms over the land, watched by countless bats that serve Strahd's will. It serves not only as Strahd's lair but as a maze of traps, undead, and dark magic meant to deter intruders.
