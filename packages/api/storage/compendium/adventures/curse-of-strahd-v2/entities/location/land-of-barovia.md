The Land of Barovia is a dark realm steeped in sorrow and fear, ruled by the vampire Strahd von Zarovich. Strahd conquered this land centuries ago, using sinister creatures to observe the inhabitants. This place is known for its deep-rooted mysteries, haunting woods, and the Old Svalich Road that connects the settlements of Krezk, Vallaki, and Barovia.

Vistani also possess knowledge and superstitions important to their culture, like the belief that souls of the dead cannot escape Barovia, and several curses that can be invoked by them.
