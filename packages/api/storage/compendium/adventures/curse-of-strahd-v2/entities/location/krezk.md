West of the two mountains, atop a hill, stands the Abbey of Saint Markovia, around which the Barovians built a walled village named Krezk.

> The fortified village of Krezk lies at the west end of the valley and is built around an old abbey. The village burgomaster is named Dmitri Krezkov.

Krezk is a sanctuary for Barovians seeking refuge from the darkness outside. Its strong walls and sense of community provide a glimmer of hope amidst despair, and its inhabitants work together to protect their way of life. However, the looming presence of Strahd remains a constant threat to their existence.
