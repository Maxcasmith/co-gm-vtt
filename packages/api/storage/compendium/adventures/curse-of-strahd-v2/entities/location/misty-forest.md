>>The Misty Forest is a dark and twisted expanse filled with thick fog that swallows the unwary. On nights of the full moon, it is home to a pack of werewolves that threaten the nearby settlements. The forest acts as a barrier and a connector between the realms and serves as a hunting ground for supernatural beasts.

## DM Notes
Navigating this forest is perilous, with many paths leading back to Strahd's domain.
