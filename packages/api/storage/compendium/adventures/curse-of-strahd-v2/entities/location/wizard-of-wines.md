>> Wine is the lifeblood of the Barovian people. It is one of the only indulgences left to them. Without it, many Barovians would lose their last shred of hope and succumb to utter despair.
The Wizard of Wines winery and vineyard is famed for producing magical wines vital to the sustenance of the local populace. Protected by the Martikov family, the winery faces constant threats but remains a symbol of resilience against darkness.
