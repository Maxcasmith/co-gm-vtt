On the south side of the lake rests the town of Vallaki, enclosed by a palisade.

> The town of Vallaki lies in the heart of the valley. Its burgomaster is named Baron Vargas Vallakovich.

Vallaki serves as a central hub within Barovia, where the baron's desire for order often clashes with the chaotic nature of the land. This town hosts various gatherings and social events in an attempt to uplift spirits but is ultimately governed by fear of Strahd and the supernatural terrors that abound.
