>>Argynvostholt is a ruined castle that was once home to a noble order of paladins. The spectral remains of the defenders linger here, protecting the legacy of their fallen comrades as they continue to battle the darkness of Strahd.

Between Vallaki and Krezk lie the ruins of Argynvostholt, the fallen bastion of a knightly order called the Order of the Silver Dragon, wiped out by Strahd and his army.
