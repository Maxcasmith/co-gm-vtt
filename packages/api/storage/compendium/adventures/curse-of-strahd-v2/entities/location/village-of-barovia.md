>>The village of Barovia cowers in the shadow of Castle Ravenloft, Strahd's home and fortress. The castle stands atop a great spire of rock, invincible and ever watchful. Every night, thousands of bats fly out of the castle to feed.

The Village of Barovia is bleak and oppressed, filled with despair as its inhabitants live under the constant threat of Strahd. Many shops are closed, and the townsfolk have surrendered to hopelessness, making it a prime location for adventurers to gather information and plan their next moves against Strahd.

## DM Notes
The village has many secrets hidden within it and can serve as a point of interest for players looking to find allies, gather resources, or uncover insights into Strahd's ongoing manipulation.

>>The Village of Barovia is cloaked in a constant fog, surrounded by the Balinok Mountains, and serves as a refuge for the native Barovians living in fear under the shadow of Strahd. It is a place of bleak stone buildings and dark histories, where the oppressed gather and conspire against their eternal foe.
