>>A fence of wrought iron with a rusty gate encloses a rectangular plot of land behind the dilapidated church. Tightly packed gravestones shrouded by fog bear the names of souls long passed. All seems quiet.

The cemetery is a place of rest for the deceased residents of Barovia. However, at midnight, it becomes the site for a ghostly procession of those who have passed on, returning to the land to fulfill their restless nature.
