The Town of Vallaki is a fortified settlement that stands against the threats posed by Strahd and his minions. Despite its defenses, it struggles with its own internal issues, and the perception of safety is often misleading. The townsfolk are wary and suspicious, which can create tension for adventurers looking for shelter or information in their quest.

## DM Notes
The town serves as a hub for gathering information about Strahd and his servants, and players may find themselves entwined in local politics.
