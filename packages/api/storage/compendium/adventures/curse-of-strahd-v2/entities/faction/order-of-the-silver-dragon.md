The Order of the Silver Dragon was founded by Lord Argynvost to recruit champions of good in their fight against evil. They were committed to defending the valley and safeguarding the Amber Temple from malevolent forces. Although they were initially successful, the order ultimately fell to Strahd's overwhelming might.

The Order of the Silver Dragon was an honorable group of knights led by Argynvost that defended Barovia from evil, particularly from Strahd. After their downfall, their spirits haunt Argynvostholt, yearning for redemption.
