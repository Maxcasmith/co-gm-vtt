The Vistani are a mysterious group known for their fortune-telling and knowledge about the land of Barovia. They often play both sides of conflicts to maintain power and influence, ensuring their survival amidst the dark powers that rule the land.

The Vistani are a group of colorful nomads who travel in covered wagons. They strike a balance between being allies and subjects of Strahd, holding knowledge about his realm but also suffering under his dark influence. Their actions and interactions with adventurers can be both supportive and treacherous.

## DM Notes
The Vistani possess potions to help travel through Strahd's domain, but they are often false and serve only to deceive.
