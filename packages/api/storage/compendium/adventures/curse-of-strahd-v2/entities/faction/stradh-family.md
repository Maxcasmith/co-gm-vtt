Strahd's family, particularly Strahd von Zarovich, has a long history of ruling Barovia and ensuring that the dark secrets of the land remain hidden. Their influence extends throughout the lands, governing both the living and the dead.
## DM Notes
The family dynamics and hidden betrayals within the family can create interesting plot twists and reveals.
